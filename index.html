About

I am a Professor (Grade 1) in the School of Computer Science & Engineering at Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT), Vellore, Tamil Nadu, India. I joined VIT in April 2010 and have progressively advanced from Assistant Professor to the current role.

My doctoral research at VIT University (awarded 26 June 2018) was in cloud security and access control. Since then I have built an active research programme in AI-driven healthcare, cybersecurity, misinformation dynamics, digital twins, and sustainable edge/cloud computing, collaborating with researchers internationally and within India.

Over 90% of my publications are available in Open Access venues (IEEE Access, Scientific Reports, MDPI). I strongly advocate Open Science practices including preprint sharing, dataset/code availability, and reproducible methodologies.

Prior to VIT I served as Assistant Professor at Union Christian College, Kerala (2006–2010), Lecturer at M.A. College of Engineering, Kerala (2001–2002), and Software Engineer at Aggies InfoTech Pvt. Ltd. (2000–2001). Note: Earlier publications are indexed as "Shynu P G"; all works are uniformly cited here as "Shynu Padinjappurathu Gopalan".
