Research Vision & Patents

My overarching goal is to build intelligent, trustworthy, and privacy-preserving systems that operate reliably in safety-critical domains[cite: 8]. I pursue this through three converging threads: AI-driven threat detection, explainable/federated ML for digital health, and misinformation dynamics[cite: 9, 10, 11].
